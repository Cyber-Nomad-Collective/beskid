## Model at a glance

`Lowering contract` is modeled as a contract surface between producer crates and consumer crates. New contributors should treat it as a boundary document first and an implementation detail map second.

## Primary actors

- Producer side: crates that define or emit the contract surface.
- Consumer side: crates that read, execute, or validate that surface.
- Conformance side: test crates that pin expected behavior.

## Concrete crate anchors

- `beskid_codegen::lower_source` in `compiler/crates/beskid_codegen`
- `CodegenArtifact` construction in `compiler/crates/beskid_codegen`
- `JitModule` consumption in `compiler/crates/beskid_engine/src/jit_module.rs`
- Runtime execution coverage in `compiler/crates/beskid_tests/src/runtime/jit.rs`
